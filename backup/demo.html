My Journey

I was born in California & raised with Aloha
+
Growing up between two worlds shaped my perspective on design and creativity. The laid-back beach culture of California mixed with the warm, community-focused spirit of Hawaiian Aloha taught me to approach every project with authenticity and heart.
I grew up playing Nintendo, reading comic books & watching the 49ers
+
These childhood passions instilled in me a love for storytelling, visual narratives, and the power of iconic design. From pixel art to comic panels to team logos, I learned that great design creates emotional connections and lasting memories.
I rode a skateboard, wore Stüssy & listened to Wu-Tang
+
In 1993, I bought my first skateboard—and in many ways, it changed everything. It became more than a way to move through the world; it was a form of expression, freedom, and identity. Through skateboarding, I found community, confidence, and a creative outlet that would inform my approach to design for years to come.
I worked my way up from an intern to Executive Creative Director
+
My career journey taught me that great design comes from curiosity, persistence, and always being willing to learn. Starting from the bottom gave me respect for every role in the creative process and shaped my collaborative approach to leadership.
Now I'm an Independent Designer & Executive Creative Director
+
Today, I bring together decades of experience to help brands tell their stories through bold, authentic design. I work with companies who value creativity, craftsmanship, and the power of visual communication to connect with audiences in meaningful ways.
01

Creative Excellence

We bring bold ideas to life through innovative design and strategic thinking. Our approach combines aesthetic excellence with functional purpose, creating experiences that resonate with audiences and drive meaningful engagement.

02

Creating immersive digital environments that captivate audiences and deliver memorable user experiences across all platforms. From web to mobile, we craft digital solutions that push boundaries and set new standards.

03

Brand Identity

Developing distinctive brand identities that resonate with audiences and stand out in today's competitive landscape. We create cohesive visual languages that tell compelling stories and build lasting connections.

04

Motion & Interactive

Bringing static designs to life through motion graphics and interactive elements that engage and inspire. Our animation work adds depth and dimension to digital experiences, creating moments of delight and wonder.
